# KAVANA to Join China's National AI Content-Production Training for County Media

KAVANA will attend the National Public-Interest Training Program on AI-Empowered Content Production for County-Level Media, hosted by the County Media Committee of the China Television Artists Association. The program runs July 8-11, 2026, in Taihu County, Anhui, China.

The training is fully free and public-interest, aimed at frontline county-media teams. Across two full days of hands-on sessions and case studies, it covers AI news topic selection, scriptwriting, multi-platform text-and-image adaptation, promotional and short-video production, smart editing, digital-human applications, and content compliance review.

As a technology team that has served China's broadcast industry for years, KAVANA will be on-site to exchange and learn with peers. Drawing on our work across 500+ radio stations and media centers, we will share practical approaches to applying AI in content production: AI-assisted writing, short-video repurposing, digital-human presenting, voice cloning, and compliance review.

We look forward to meeting fellow county-media professionals on-site.
